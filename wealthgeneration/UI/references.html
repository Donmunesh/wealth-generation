How It Works

For us to grow, invite others!

Registration

During registration, make sure you use your correct mobile number. This is how other members can communicate with you when you are getting paid.

After registration, Proceed to request for shares

Login

After your registration fee is confirmed, you will be able to login to your account, otherwise you will be stuck on the registration payment page.

To login your account, you will need your username and password. Kindly chat with online support or group admins when you lose or forget Your password.

Invest

When you login to your account after registration, on your dashboard click on invest. On the box provided, input your desired investment amount.

Note that minimum amount to invest is 500ksh and the maximum amount is 5000ksh.

Timer

Immediately you are comfirmed, your timer for 2 days will start counting down.

During these period, you will be allocated a receiver.

Make Payment

After your investment is allocated to a receiver, all you need to do is to login to your account, make payment and upload your proof of payment.

You are also required (or not) to call the recipient for quick confirmation of your payment.

Note that you will be accorded 3 hours to Make payment. Otherwise, your account will be blocked and pending investment(s) will be cancelled.

Get Paid

When your timer is over, a cashout button will be visible, click on it, you will be allocated a payer with 30% interest.

Make sure you confirm on the site when you receive your payment.

Referral

Wealth Generation offers 5% continous referral referral bonus. Whenever your downlines invest, you get paid. The referral bonus will be added to your incoming funds, meaning you have to invest in order to be paid the bonus. Kindly note that your accumulated referral bonus must be greater or equal to your investment otherwise the bonus will not be paid.

Guiders

In Wealth Generation, we appreciate and encourage our members to invite other members (as this is how our community will grow). Therefore we introduce a guider system. Guiders are members who have 20 active downlines or above. To show our appreciation, we offer our guiders incentives. Guiders get extra 10% incentives of their investment. Meaning when guiders invest, they get 40% instead of the regular 30%. Guiders also get registration fee from new members. To be eligible to be a guider, you must have an investment of 30,000ksh and a minimum of 20 active downlines.
